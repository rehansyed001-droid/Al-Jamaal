About Aljamaal

Our Story

Aljamaal Official was established in 2018 by a family with a shared passion for modest, elegant fashion. What began as a local operation in Johannesburg has grown into a recognised South African clothing brand — built on word of mouth, community trust, and garments people genuinely love to wear.

The name "Aljamaal" (الجمال) means beauty in Arabic. It's the idea at the core of everything we make — that clothing should honour the wearer, celebrate culture, and be crafted to last. From our signature Original Burnoose — a garment rooted in Sunnah, worn by our beloved Nabi ﷺ — to our ladies burqas and ¾ shorts, every piece reflects that belief.

We are proudly South African, proudly family-run, and proud to serve a community that values quality, identity, and faith-inspired style. Whether you're shopping for yourself or your family, Aljamaal is made for you.
